Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I enable the keyboard?
A: Go to Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Add New Keyboard → AIKeyboard, then allow Full Access (optional).
Q: Can I subscribe or cancel at any time?
A: Yes, subscription management is done via your App Store account.
Q: What data does the keyboard collect?
A: The keyboard does not collect any personal data unless explicitly authorized by you. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
